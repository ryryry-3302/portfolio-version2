### Introduction

Leasy is a specialized lease takeover platform designed for the dynamic housing needs of students, interns, and exchange students in San Francisco. It simplifies the process of finding short-term sublets and lease takeovers in a competitive market.

### Project Overview

Finding short-term housing in San Francisco is notoriously difficult. Leasy addresses this by providing a centralized platform specifically for lease takeovers, featuring a Zillow-like interface that makes browsing listings intuitive and efficient.

### Key Features

- **Interactive Map View:** Integrated Mapbox GL JS to provide a seamless, interactive map experience for exploring listings by location.
- **Advanced Filtering:** Users can filter listings by price range, number of bedrooms, and availability dates to find their perfect match.
- **Responsive Design:** Fully responsive interface that works flawlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.
- **Detailed Listings:** Comprehensive listing pages featuring photo galleries, amenities, and lease terms.
- **Mock Data:** Populated with 15+ realistic mock listings in popular neighborhoods like SOMA and the Mission to demonstrate functionality.

### Technology Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router) with TypeScript
- **Maps:** Mapbox GL JS
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Icons:** Lucide React
