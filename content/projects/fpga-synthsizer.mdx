## Introduction

This project was embarked on during my internship at the National Metrology Centre of Singapore as part of the ARIA program

Currently the supported features include
- 1PPS input synchronisation
- 1PPS output generation
- UART Communications
    - Phase modulation
    - Pulse width modulation
    - Reset

I worked on this project under the supervision of Liaw Chin Yi at the National Metrology Centre
