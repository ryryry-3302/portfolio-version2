## Description
Led a team of 8 to design and build an electric vehicle from scratch, contributing to the advancement of sustainable transportation solutions. This project, part of the SST-SUTD Big Discovery Camp Program, addresses the growing need for eco-friendly alternatives in the automotive industry.

## Problems Addressed
Addressing the need for eco-friendly alternatives in the automotive industry.

-   AutoCAD for chassis design
-   Electronics assembly and integration
-   Mechanical engineering principles
-   Collaborative project management
-   Prototyping and testing
